About the Artist

Rajithha Reddy

Welcome to Vennela Makeover Studio, led by Rajithha Reddy — a professional bridal makeup artist based in Armoor, Telangana. With a passion for transforming brides into their most beautiful selves, I specialize in bridal makeup, mehendi artistry, hair styling, and expert saree draping that blends tradition with modern elegance.

Beyond the studio, I also run Vennela Academy and Boutique, where I train aspiring makeup artists and offer curated fashion collections. Every bride who walks into Vennela Makeover Studio receives a personalized, flawless look crafted with care and dedication for her most special day.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you provide on-location services? +

Yes! We provide on-location bridal makeup services across Armoor, Nizamabad, Hyderabad, and nearby areas. Travel charges may apply for distant locations.

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for bridal services, especially during wedding season (October-February). For other events, 2-3 weeks notice is ideal.

Do you offer bridal trials? +

Yes! Bridal trials are included in our Bridal Package. This helps us understand your preferences and ensure you're 100% happy with your wedding day look.

What products do you use? +

We use high-quality, long-lasting professional makeup brands that are suitable for all skin types. Products include MAC, Huda Beauty, Kryolan, and premium Indian brands.

Can you do makeup for the entire bridal party? +

Absolutely! We can accommodate bridesmaids, mothers, and other family members. Contact us for group pricing and packages.
